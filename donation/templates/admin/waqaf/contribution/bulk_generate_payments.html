How Payment Schedules Work:

  • One-off Contributions: Single payment, no schedule needed
  • Recurring Contributions: Multiple payments over time
  • Payment Schedules: Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, or Yearly
  • Auto-generation: Creates individual Payment records for each installment
  • First Payment: Automatically marked as completed
  • Subsequent Payments: Marked as pending with due dates
