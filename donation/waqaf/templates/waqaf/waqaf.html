About Waqaf

What is Waqaf?

Waqaf is an Islamic endowment of property to be held in trust and used for charitable or religious purposes. It is a form of continuous charity that benefits the community.

Benefits of Waqaf

Waqaf properties provide sustainable benefits to the community, including education, healthcare, and religious services. Your contribution creates lasting impact.
